Stay Connected

Join crypto-enthusiasts, entrepreneurs, blockchain startups, and freelancers in the Full Node open co-working area. You can reserve a fixed desk of your own or choose an open seat and start working.

Build your Block

Wanna close the door from time to time? Our lockable, private offices surrounded by glass walls can accommodate teams of 2, 4, or 6 people. They are fully furnished and include personal desks, ergonomic chairs, cabinets, whiteboards, and more.

Dream Big

Inspiration needed? We got you covered: From playful lounges, brainstorming rooms, and phone booths to audio boxes where you can record your podcast, there's enough space for you to build the blocks of your project. Need a power nap? Climb up to the sleep box and recharge.
